Nullius in verba — take nobody's word for it.

AI models plan research, write and execute analysis code, and draft a manuscript on your machine. Deterministic gates make sure no number and no citation enters the report unless it can be traced to real evidence: sandbox-executed artifacts, Crossref-verified references, reproducible runs.
